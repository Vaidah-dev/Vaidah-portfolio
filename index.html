Cape Town-A City I Love

Capetown is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. Surrounded by mountains and oceans it blend nature, culture and history in a way that feels unique every time you explore it.

It offers variety, from sunsets at numerous beaches to hiking the table mountain, there is always some breathtaking views.

The city is also known for its guaranteed awesomeness diverse foods, friendliest people,a vibrant lifestyle and amazing views. I could not think of a better city to live in.

Things I Love About Cape Town

  • Beautiful beaches and sunsets
  • Table Mountain views
  • Adventures
  • Amazing food spots
  • Rich culture and history
